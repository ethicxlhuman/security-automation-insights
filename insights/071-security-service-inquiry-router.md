# Security Service Inquiry Router 

A multi-step qualification router for cybersecurity companies that maps prospect
answers to the most relevant service offering and surfaces a clear recommended
next step.

## The Problem

Security company websites lose qualified prospects because:
- Service pages list everything, explain nothing
- Visitors self-select the wrong tier (over/under qualified)
- No follow-through: visitors bounce without booking or contacting
- MDR, GRC, and pentest buyers have completely different needs and budgets

**Result:** High traffic, low conversion. Sales team chases cold leads.

## The Solution

A 3-step qualification flow that:
- Asks role, company size, and primary security need
- Maps answer combinations to the correct service lane (MDR, GRC, Offensive Security, etc.)
- Surfaces a single recommended next step (book a call, view a case study, download a spec sheet)
- Preserves answers across steps so context is never lost

## Use Cases

- MDR/SOC providers routing SMB vs. enterprise prospects
- GRC firms separating SOC 2 vs. ISO 27001 vs. HIPAA inquiry paths
- Pentest shops qualifying web app vs. cloud vs. red team buyers
- Multi-service security agencies reducing discovery call time

## Impact

- Reduces unqualified discovery calls by routing prospects pre-meeting
- Saves 30-60 min per week of founder/sales time on intake filtering
- Increases conversion by matching visitor to relevant offer immediately
