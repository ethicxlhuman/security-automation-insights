# Service area qualifier 

An interactive service coverage checker for cybersecurity firm websites that
instantly qualifies or disqualifies prospects based on geographic scope, before
they book a discovery call or submit a contact form.

## The Problem

MDR and SOC-as-a-Service providers operate within defined geographic or
regulatory boundaries. Without a coverage gate:

- Sales teams waste 30-45 min discovery calls on out-of-scope prospects
- CRM pipelines fill with unqualified leads
- Ops teams field repetitive "do you cover X?" support emails

## The Solution

A client-side coverage checker that:
- Accepts postcode, city, or area name as input
- Normalizes input (lowercase, trim, partial match) against a local coverage list
- Instantly renders a covered or not-covered state
- Swaps the CTA dynamically: "Book a Call" vs. "Join Waitlist" or "See Partners"

## Use Cases

- MDR firms qualifying enterprise clients by region or jurisdiction
- SOC-as-a-Service providers with tiered geographic coverage
- GRC consultancies with compliance scope tied to specific regulatory zones (HIPAA, GDPR)
- MSSPs screening SMB prospects before routing to the right delivery team

## Impact

- Eliminates unqualified discovery calls before they hit the calendar
- Reduces inbound ops noise by 40-60% for regionally scoped providers
- Improves lead quality in CRM without adding a manual qualification step
